Welcome!

Hi, I'm Aidan.

I'm an Amazon bestselling thriller author originally from Cloughmills, Northern Ireland.

As of August 2019, I have written four novels. PATHFINDERS (sci-fi), RAISING LAZARUS (suspense) and the Tom Regan thrillers, SIGIL and YAGE. I have also written several short stories and I'm most proud of the ROT series, which tells the story of a rogue tooth fairy who pulls teeth out of the mouths of kids for a tidy profit.

You can follow me on my social platforms (see footer) or get a bunch of free goodies by following my newsletter.
