Bio

AIDAN J. REID is a thriller author originally from Cloughmills, Northern Ireland.

As of May 2019, he has written four novels. PATHFINDERS (sci-fi), RAISING LAZARUS (suspense) and the Tom Regan Thrillers, SIGIL and YAGE. He has also written several short stories and is most proud of the ROT collection, which tells the story of a rogue tooth fairy who pulls teeth out of the mouths of kids for a tidy profit.

You can find out more on aidanjreid.com, follow him @aidanjreid or get a bunch of free goodies by following his newsletter - http://eepurl.com/c7ffO9
